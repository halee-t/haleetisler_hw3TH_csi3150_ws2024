Something, Cacti & Canyons with Trails from Page

Your Something, Cacti, and Canyons adventures starts with a scenic dune buggie ride through the hills of Page. The buggie departs from the Page Depot located at 123 West First Avenue at 9:45am. After traveling through Page you will descend through the Page Canyon Wildlife and Nature Refuge.

What To Wear

Your trip includes professional guides, raingear, water canteens and a small snack. Dress in athletic gear as we will be hiking through the canyons. Please bring a hat (to protect your head and face from the sun), appropriate layers (it will be a hot one! But some trails can get chilly), sunscreen, and tennis sheos. You may wish to bring binoculars and a camera if you have them.
